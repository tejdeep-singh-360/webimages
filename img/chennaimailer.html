As we all know that since Oct 2012 as per NABL guidelines, it has become mandatory for the quality manager of a lab, to get trained in an internal auditor’s course for ISO-15189 before applying for NABL accreditation. It is also essential for the pathologists, microbiologists, biochemists & technicians, of all the clinical labs who want to follow quality protocols, to understand this international standard. With increasing demand, we felt the need of conducting internal auditor’s course at your doorstep.

This course is designed keeping in mind requirements of NABL that all NABL accredited/to be accredited laboratories are required to comply with revised and new standard i.e. ISO 15189:2012 before October 2015. All laboratories accredited till date as per previous version are required to ensure compliance with the revised version when they are reassessed during scheduled Surveillance/Reassessment audits or as a separate assessment.

For the convenience of the participants these courses are being organized under a program named "Health Education & Learning Protocols" (HELP) in collaboration with "Quality Council of India" under the guidance of Dr.Ms.J. Bhatia (NABL Assessor and member of Quality Council of India).



Candidates will get the certificate of completion of the course.
